Essential Cookies
Essential cookies are a type of cookie that are essential for the proper functioning of a website. They are also known as "strictly necessary cookies" or "essential cookies". These cookies are used to store information that is needed for a website to work properly, such as login credentials, shopping cart information, and language preferences. Without necessary cookies, some features of a website may not be available, and the website may not function as intended.
Analytics & Statistics
My analytic cookies are used to analyze and measure website usage, such as which pages are visited most frequently, how long visitors stay on each page, and how visitors navigate through the site.
Analytical cookies help website owners understand how their website is being used and identify areas for improvement.
Marketing & Social
My marketing cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit us. These cookies are used by advertisers and marketers to track user behavior across the web and to deliver personalized ads based on the user's interests and preferences.
This information is then used to build a profile of the user's interests and behaviors, which can be used to deliver targeted advertising.
